Help & User Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to 9A1 Quiz! This platform allows teachers to create quizzes and students to practice with flashcards and take quizzes.

For Teachers

Creating an Account

  1. Click 'Sign Up' in the top navigation
  2. Select 'Teacher' as your role
  3. Enter the teacher code: TEACHER2025
  4. Fill in your username, password, and select your avatar

Creating Quizzes

  1. Log in and go to Activities page
  2. Scroll down to 'Create a New Quiz' section
  3. Enter your quiz title
  4. Add questions using 'Add Question' button
  5. For multiple choice: enter options and select the correct answer
  6. Click 'Create Quiz' when done

Viewing Student Progress

Check the Dashboard section on the Activities page to see:

  • Number of quizzes created
  • Total student attempts
  • Average accuracy rate
  • Top performing students

For Students

Creating an Account

  1. Click 'Sign Up' in the top navigation
  2. Select 'Student' as your role
  3. Fill in your username, password, and select your avatar

Taking Quizzes

  1. Log in and go to Activities page
  2. Browse available quizzes created by teachers
  3. Click 'Start Quiz' on any quiz
  4. Answer questions and click 'Next' to proceed
  5. View your results at the end

Using Flashcards

  1. Go to the Flashcard page
  2. Add new cards using the form on the right
  3. Click on cards to flip them and see the answer
  4. Use Previous/Next buttons to navigate

Tips & Best Practices

For Teachers

  • Create clear, concise questions
  • Use multiple choice for quick assessment
  • Add essay questions for deeper thinking
  • Monitor student progress regularly

For Students

  • Take quizzes multiple times to improve
  • Use flashcards for memorization
  • Practice regularly for best results

Need More Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact your teacher or the development team.

This platform is designed to make learning interactive and engaging. Enjoy your quiz experience!
